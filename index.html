About Me

I am the girl next door and I believe I am born with a gift to potray my imagination of the beautiful life around on the canvas. Colors around me makes me happy and putting them on the canvas brings me peace. My passion for different forms of art and paintings has grown over the years and I wish to keep exploring and learning. I am showcasing my oil paintings and sketching on this website and hoping people would love my work.
